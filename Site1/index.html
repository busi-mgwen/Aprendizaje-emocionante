Who we are

Our collective wants to explore the different ways of learning.As part of this exploration we want to collect all individual learning paths of people in Barcelona through a collective reflective experience.

Our Challenge

We studied in systems created by the needs of previous generations and led by them. 
Today's needs changed and the hierarchy in teaching and learning should change as well.
How can we change what we learn and how we learn?
